Who We Are

We believe every bilingual child deserves resources that honor both of their languages. Biliteracy Bridge was founded on the belief that raising a bilingual child is one of the most powerful gifts a family can give — and that every parent deserves the tools to make it happen.

What We Do

We create premium workbooks, free resources, and expert guidance for bilingual families navigating English and Spanish literacy together — made by a parent, policy advisor, and former kindergarten teacher who has lived this journey.

What We Believe

Our guiding principles

Biliteracy is a gift

Every child who grows up reading and writing in two languages carries a lifelong advantage — cognitively, culturally, and professionally.

Parents are the bridge

Families are the most powerful force in a child's language journey. We build tools that make that journey joyful and achievable.

Both languages matter equally

We never treat Spanish as secondary. Our resources celebrate and strengthen both languages with equal care and intention.

Biliteracy Bridge was built by a parent who lived this journey — and wanted to make it easier for every family that comes after.
